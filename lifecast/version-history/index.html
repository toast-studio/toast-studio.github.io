Version History

2.0.1

23 June 2020

  • On Android the background image now extends beneath the System Bar
  • Improved the design of the launch screen
  • Added link to Version History in the Settings screen

2.0

23 February 2020

  • Android support
  • Added 2 player mode
  • Added Commander mode which allows you to track Commander damage
  • Added the ability to save custom background images
  • Added the ability to long press on the + and - buttons to quickly change your life total
  • Added a tooltip that helps you keep track when you change your life total
  • Added counters for each of the mana types to keep track of floating mana pools
  • Added dice rolls
  • Added coin flips
  • Added Artefact theme
  • Added support for large life totals
  • Improved Voiceover Support
  • Fixed the Midnight theme image to remove the white bar that was present
  • Improved the legibility of some themes

1.0

15 May 2019

  • Life counter
  • Support for Poison, Electric, Loyalty, Planeswalker, and 4 custom counters
  • Support for Standard, Modern, Commander, Brawl, and Two-Headed Giant formats
  • 18 different themes
