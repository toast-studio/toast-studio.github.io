Identifiers

We use Google Analytics as a way to get anonymised usage analytics about Screen Sizes. Please refer to the Google Analytics Privacy Policy for how your usage data is collected and stored.

We use Carbon Ads as a way to provide advertising to monetize Screen Sizes. Carbon makes use of identifiers to provide advertising, we do not have access to who these identifiers belong to. Please refer to the Carbon Ads Privacy Policy for how your data is collected and stored.

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data as a way to see how many people use Screen Sizes and in what way they are using it to help us improve Screen Sizes in the future.

Carbon Ads will collect usage data as a way to provide advertising and verify advertising metrics.

Browsing History

When you visit one of the links to other Toast apps on the Info screen we collect this information as a way to determine referrals.

Location

Your coarse location is provided to us by Google Analytics via your IP Address. We do not access your precise GPS co-ordinates. We use this information to see what countries our users are from.

Diagnostics

Google Analytics provides certain information about the device you access Screen Sizes with. This can include your operating system version, cellular network, display resolution, type of browser, etc.
