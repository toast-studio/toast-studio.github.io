Version History

1.3.5

14 September 2020

  • Fixed a crash that would happen if you didn’t give Simple Scales access to Apple Health

1.3.4

15 January 2020

  • Fixed an issue with the position of the scroller on the iPhone app

1.3.3

13 May 2019

  • Added a link to our FAQ in the Info section

1.3.2

28 December 2018

  • Added support for Apple Watch Series 4, iPhone XS Max, and iPhone XR screen sizes
  • Added support for new Infographic Circular complications on the Series 4 Watch
  • New Info screen in the iPhone app that will help you with changing your preferred weight unit, viewing our privacy policy, and getting in contact with us
  • Minimum requirements increased to iOS 11 and watchOS 5

1.3.1

23 March 2018

  • Set the current weight and BMI to monospace digits so they don't jump around as you scroll
  • Added a fallback for the watch app when previously saved weight isn’t available (this sometimes happens when the watch has cleared older data from its Apple Health store)

1.3

17 November 2017

  • Added BMI support to Watch app (enable write support for Height in Apple Health to enable this)
  • Improved layout including full support for iPhone X
  • Fixed a bug for UK users on iOS 10 where weight was not visible in the phone app
  • Fixed a bug where switching your unit in Health would not display correctly inside Simple Scales

1.2

25 September 2017

  • New design for the watch app focused on legibility
  • Performance improvements for the watch app
  • The watch app for this update only supports watchOS 4

1.1.1

17 March 2017

  • Your preferred weight unit from the Health app is now used instead of your region settings (Find this in Health > Body Measurements > Weight > Unit)
  • Added support for displaying your weight in Stones
  • Performance improvements for the Watch app

1.1

6 December 2016

  • Brand new watch app for even quicker weight entry. Includes complication for quick access
  • Improved haptics on iPhone 7 and 7 Plus
  • Improvements to layout on 4" devices
  • Visual feedback when you save your weight on the iPhone app

1.0

29 September 2016

  • Connect to Apple Health
  • View your last saved weight in Apple Health
  • Save your new weight to Apple Health
