Identifiers

We use Google Analytics as a way to get anonymised usage analytics about TypeDex. Google Analytics makes use of identifiers to tie sessions together, we do not have access to who these identifiers belong to. Please refer to the Google Analytics Privacy Policy for how your usage data is collected and stored.

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data as a way to see how many people use TypeDex and in what way they are using it to help us improve TypeDex in the future.

Location

Your coarse location is provided to us by Google Analytics via your IP Address. We do not access your precise GPS co-ordinates. We use this information to see what countries our users are from for localisation purposes.

Browsing History

When you visit one of the links to other Toast apps on the Info tab we collect this information as a way to determine referrals.

Diagnostics

Google Analytics provides certain information about the device you access TypeDex with. This can include your operating system version, cellular network, display resolution, type of browser, etc.
